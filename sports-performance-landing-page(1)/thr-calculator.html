How Heart Rate Zones Optimize Cardiovascular Rehab and Peak Performance

Heart rate is the most accessible window into cardiac output and autonomic stress. 5 Zones That Matter

Zone 1 (50-60%) Recovery: Active recovery, parasympathetic rebound, early phase cardiac rehab and post-stroke mobilization.

From Rehab to Podium
